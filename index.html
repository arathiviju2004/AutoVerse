Welcome to the Future of Auto Ride Services

Our Auto Ride Tracking System is designed to bring convenience, transparency, and safety to both passengers and drivers by digitizing the entire ride experience. With features that empower both riders and drivers, we are creating a seamless, modern solution for everyday travel.

Sign In For Customers Sign In For Drivers

Why Choose Our System?

Revolutionizing Auto Rickshaw Travel with Technology

By leveraging modern technologies such as GPS, QR codes, and digital profiles, we are modernizing the traditional auto rickshaw experience. Whether you're a passenger or a driver, our system offers features designed to make your ride safer, more efficient, and more transparent.

What Services we offer to our customers

Fare Calculation

Easily calculate your fare based on distance and government-regulated rates.

GPS Tracking

Track your ride in real-time for a secure, efficient journey.

QR Code Scanning

Simply scan the QR code at the beginning of your ride to initiate the process.

Driver Details

Know exactly who your driver is with detailed information provided before the ride starts.

Live Location Sharing

Share your live location with loved ones for added safety.

Ratings & Feedback

Rate your ride experience and provide feedback to ensure the best service.

Emergency SOS

In case of an emergency, trigger the SOS feature to alert authorities or your emergency contacts immediately.

Track Earnings

Fuel Cost Tracker

Track fuel usage and expenses efficiently.

Maintenance Reminders

Receive timely reminders for vehicle maintenance, ensuring your auto stays in optimal condition.

Ratings and Feedback

See what passengers think and improve your service.
